About Me

This page is just a small bridge; if you really want to know about me, you should go to About Page.

I have been passionate about technology since I was a child, and I have always been fascinated by the inner workings of computers. I started tinkering with old PCs and learning about Linux at a young age, and it has been a hobby of mine ever since. I enjoy bringing old PCs back to life and giving them a new purpose, whether it's for gaming, programming, or just general use.

In any case, I can say that I really like Lenovo ThinkPads, especially the X200 series, I like CRT monitors but I've never had one.
